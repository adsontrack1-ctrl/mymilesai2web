Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Harijas LLC

1. Information We Collect

We collect GPS location data, trip distances, timestamps, and classifications for mileage tracking and tax deduction calculation. We also collect your account email address for authentication.

2. Cloud Storage

Your trip data is securely stored in the cloud (Supabase / AWS), encrypted at rest and in transit. Cloud storage enables backup, multi-device sync, and account recovery. On-device caching is used for performance only.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide mileage tracking, generate tax reports, and sync your account. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with Stripe (payment processing) and Supabase (secure cloud storage). We do not sell your data.

5. Speedometer

The in-app speedometer is for reference only and is not calibrated to DMV, state, or federal standards. Do not rely on it for legal purposes.

6. Your Rights

You may export or delete all data at any time. Account deletion removes all cloud data within 30 days.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@mymilesai.com.
